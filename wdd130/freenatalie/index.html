WELCOME FELLOW DISC GOLFERS!

I play disc golf on an amateur level, competing in tournaments around the Pacific Northwest each season. One of the things that I’ve enjoyed about disc Golf is the friendliness and inclusiveness of the community. Disc golf is a sport that anyone can enjoy. It makes me sad that anyone would feel like they are being excluded or pushed out from something that they are passionate about. I know people have a lot of questions and concerns about transgender athletes participating in sports. As a fan of Natalie and a fellow PDGA member, I hope to provide information that will help to answer some of these questions and show my support for Natalie Ryan and other transgender athletes who wish to participate in disc golf.

LEARN MORE

Come to my website to find information about Natalie Ryan’s involvement with the Professional Disc Golf Association (PDGA). Natalie is a trans woman who has been competing in professional disc golf in the women’s division since 2018 and has been sponsored by Neptune Discs. Recently, there has been a lot of controversy about her participation and the PDGA has taken a stance that does not allow Natalie to continue to play in the women’s professional division any longer.

Several months ago, the PDGA sent out a questionnaire to it’s members asking for their opinion on the subject of transgender athletes. I believe that misinformation and fear caused many members to express that they were against transgender participation. I would like PDGA members to be aware of what is going on and to have all of the information that they need in order to be educated about the issues and voice their opinion based on fact instead of fear. I hope that people will come to my site because I am not a transgender athlete, but I am PDGA member who is concerned about this issue, so I hope that I can provide a viewpoint that is neutral and answers the questions that people have.
